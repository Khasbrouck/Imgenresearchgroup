Outstanding Student Researcher

Student Research Spotlight The first student spotlight will be on Alex Asturias, DO, who recently was first author on a publication cited below:

Alexander Asturias is a former research intern and current consulting researcher for Imgen. Alex presently holds a DO from Touro University Nevada as well as a master’s in Neuroimaging and Informatics from University of Southern California. He completed his residency at Southern Hills Hospital and is currently pursuing advanced training in diagnostic radiology as part of the clinician scientist track at UT Southwestern Medical Center. Dr. Asturias is an expert in diffusion tensor image processing and acquisition, contributing to cutting-edge medical research and practice here at Imgen.
